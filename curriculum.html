Curriculum

Curriculum

Playway method of learning is a child-centric method where the method of teaching is informal and natural to suit the child’s interests. Our belief learning is best through play activities.

We make teaching more lively and interesting. We teach languages, mathematics, social studies etc through a series of activities like games, songs, free play, gardening, construction activities etc. The learning is not just limited to cognitive development, but also for the overall development of the child. A few methods and techniques included in this method are Role plays, Projects; Theme based learning, Field trips, Puppetry etc.

At our schools we also follow some level of formal learning as well, to ensure that the child is ready for the primary education.

Your child will receive complete freedom of expression and we will mold them into beautiful young minds, which have a keen interest in knowing about things, and how do they work through entertaining activities, sports, numerical, alphabet studies and a lot more to weld in a creative world.

Methods adopted in teaching include both modern and ancient. Children’s natural intelligence and learning styles are recognised to educate them effectively.

Best in class curriculum

Association with XSEED - one of the best education service providers, we follow textbooks with the most relevant and useful content for children with different age-group.

XSEED is a proven research-based academic program that builds thinking skills and problem-solving confidence in children. XSEED children ask more questions, are not afraid to speak up in English, persist longer in solving problems and score well on tests.

A learning classroom, the 5-step teaching method

We teach child based on a scientific 5-step learning process. Teachers begin a class by clearly stating the learning goal, demonstrate an experiment or conduct a group activity, let children speak before they do, ensure the application questions are done independently, assess daily and support each child individually.

360-degree Child development

Overall development of children is the core idea. Utmost priority to different skills of language and communication development of numerical abilities at and crafts and soft skills.
