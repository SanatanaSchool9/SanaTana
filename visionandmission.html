Vision & Mission


Vision & Mission

Our goal is to provide a structured curriculum and a positive environment which is designed to meet social, emotional, language and cognitive development of children and help them accept the challenge to succeed throughout their school years and beyond.


We also aim to partner with parents to see a generation emerge from our school, as a person who know their identity a purpose in life and share the freedom, hope a love in our community, nation and world over. We envision our children to become active participants in, and shapers of, their worlds and to grow cognitively, socially, emotionally and globally through child based, teacher facilitated learning.
